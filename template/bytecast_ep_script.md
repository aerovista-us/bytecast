## Quick Tutorial: How to Use This Page (45–60s)
Here’s how you drive it:
- Press **Space** to play and pause.
- Use **J** and **L** to jump back/forward ten seconds.
- Press **M** to mark moments you want to remember.
- Use the **left/right arrows** to move through the slides.

The goal is simple: no doom scrolling.
You move forward, one layer at a time.

## The AeroVista Loop (60–75s)

It’s not “build forever.”
It’s **ship → learn → sharpen → ship again**.
