# Fulfillment SOP (Internal)

## Standard phases
1) Intake + deposit + assets
2) Direction lock (scope, timeline, deliverables)
3) Build/produce
4) Review round 1
5) Review round 2 (standard final)
6) Delivery + handoff + archive

## Revision policy (default)
- Two rounds included.
- Additional changes become a small add-on or a change order.

## Standard handoff
- Final deliverables
- “What changed” summary
- Maintenance notes / SOP
- Any credentials/access returned to client control
