# The Art Localized – Phase 1 Manifesto

AI doesn’t replace artists—it localizes creativity.

AeroVista’s vision is to empower local and regional artists by using AI as a creative amplifier. The Art Localized declares that creative equity belongs to the people who shape their communities.

Core Message:
- Empowerment: local creators gain studio-level power.
- Authenticity: regional stories deserve visibility.
- Efficiency with Soul: AI enhances art without stripping it of humanity.
- Resistance to Homogenization: counterpoint to corporate sameness.
- Collaboration over Replacement: machines amplify, not overwrite, human intent.

Promise:
AeroVista will lead a movement where tech amplifies locality—not erases it.
