Phase 3 Overview: Amplification Protocol

Objective: Scale “The Art Localized” from a community initiative to a multi-division, partnership-driven movement amplifying local creativity worldwide.

Strategy Overview:

  1. Partnerships: Form alliances with galleries, universities, maker spaces, and tech companies.
  2. Division Integration: Synchronize Lumina, EchoVerse, SkyForge, Vespera, Summit, and Horizon to amplify impact.
  3. Media Expansion: Press coverage, documentary, and digital ads to reach global audiences.
  4. Analytics Dashboard: Launch the AeroVista Creative Equity Index to measure creator reach and engagement.

Key Results:

  • 50+ partnerships established
  • 250K+ total reach
  • 500+ active creators
  • 10K+ transactions via creative marketplace

Tagline: “Amplify the local. Resonate the global.”
