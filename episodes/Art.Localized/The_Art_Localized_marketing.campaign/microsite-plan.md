# 🌐 The Art Localized Microsite – Public Campaign Portal

**Domain:** theartlocalized.com (or aerovista.us/localized)

## Sections
1. **Hero Section** – ‘AI didn’t take the art. It gave it back.’ (Video + Call to Action)
2. **Our Mission** – Explain campaign purpose and redistribution of opportunity.
3. **Artist Stories** – Short testimonials and feature reels.
4. **Marketplace Preview** – Showcases creative works and AeroVista ecosystem tools.
5. **Join the Movement** – Creator signup + newsletter form.

## Technical Stack
- Frontend: Next.js + TailwindCSS  
- Backend: Firebase (Auth + RTDB + Storage)  
- Analytics: Plausible + AeroVista Metrics API
