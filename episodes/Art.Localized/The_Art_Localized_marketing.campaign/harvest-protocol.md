**Division:** Lumina Creative Media (Lead)  
**Supporting:** Nexus TechWorks • Summit Learning • Vespera Publishing

Establish long-term sustainability, measure impact, and ensure creative equity remains accessible for all participants.

2. **Impact Report** — Documents outcomes: funded artists, new collaborations, and community feedback.  
3. **Legacy Framework** — Develops regional AeroVista Hubs for ongoing workshops and creator incubation.

- 5,000+ creators onboarded by end of cycle  
- 20 regional partnerships formed  
- 85% of creators reporting higher income or reach
