# 🎓 Summit Learning – AI for Local Creators Curriculum

**Goal:** Equip artists with accessible, ethical AI tools to elevate their craft.

## Course Modules
1. **Creative Foundations** – Understanding AI as a collaborator.  
2. **Practical Tools** – Photoshop, MusicMaker, Canva, Runway ML, etc.  
3. **Ethical AI** – Copyright, authorship, and data transparency.  
4. **Build & Monetize** – Portfolio setup, branding, and revenue streams.

**Format:**  
- 5-week micro-course (online & hybrid)  
- Certificate: “AeroVista Certified Local Creator”
