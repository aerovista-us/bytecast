# 🧩 Regional Ambassador Program – The Art Localized

## Mission
Empower real creators as campaign representatives in their communities.

## Structure
- **Ambassadors:** Local artists, educators, and community leaders.  
- **Responsibilities:** Host events, share stories, represent AeroVista locally.  
- **Perks:** Profile features, travel stipends, exclusive workshops.

## Target Regions
- Pacific Northwest (USA)
- Metro Manila (PH)
- Ontario (CA)
- Barcelona (ES)

## Motto
“Many voices. One vision.”
