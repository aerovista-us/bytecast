The Art Localized - Phase 2 Developer Pack

Phase 2 Overview: Growth Protocol

Objective: Expand from narrative awareness to creator activation. Build a network of regional artists under AeroVista’s shared identity of “localized creativity.”

Core Components:

  • Artist Onboarding: Launch the AeroVista Artist Network on `/the-art-localized/join`.
  • Spotlight Series: Weekly features on creators using AI in local art.
  • Sound + Vision Sessions: Live AI + Art showcases via EchoVerse.
  • Submission Portal: Accept artwork, remixes, and video collaborations.

Metrics:

  • 100+ artist signups in 60 days
  • 10k+ weekly impressions
  • 5 regional showcases
  • 1 partner per region (gallery or maker space)

Tagline: “From our neighborhoods to the network — creativity belongs to everyone.”

Artist Onboarding Flow

  1. Landing Page: Hosted on `/the-art-localized/join` with a simple intake form for name, region, medium, AI tools used, and sample uploads.
  2. Verification: The Lumina team reviews submissions weekly and issues a “Seed Artist” badge for approved profiles.
  3. Engagement: Artists receive an email welcome pack with templates, a brand kit, and promotional materials, along with access to a Discord/Slack channel for regional collaboration.
  4. Progression: Artists can progress from Seed Artist to Regional Beacon to AeroVista Partner, with opportunities for EchoVerse collaborations and merchandising.

Spotlight Framework

A weekly video and article featuring a regional AI-powered creator.

Format:

  • 30-second reel (mobile-optimized)
  • Long-form interview or blog (Vespera Publishing)
  • Music track (EchoVerse remix)

Initial Regions:

  1. Pacific Northwest
  2. Midwest
  3. Northeast
  4. Southeast
  5. Philippines

Deliverables:

  • Artist quote graphic (1080x1080)
  • Split-screen edit: analog + digital art
  • Hashtag set: #TheArtLocalized #AeroVista #LuminaCreative
