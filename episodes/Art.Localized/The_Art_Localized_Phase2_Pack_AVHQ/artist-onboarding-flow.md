# 🎨 Artist Onboarding Flow

## Step 1 – Landing Page
- Hosted on `/the-art-localized/join`
- Simple intake form: name, region, medium, AI tools used, sample upload
- Optional: profile photo + social handles

## Step 2 – Verification
- Lumina team reviews submissions weekly.
- “Seed Artist” badge issued for approved profiles.

## Step 3 – Engagement
- Email welcome pack with templates, brand kit, and promo materials.
- Access to Discord/Slack channel for regional collaboration.

## Step 4 – Progression
- **Seed Artist** → **Regional Beacon** → **AeroVista Partner**
- Featured creators eligible for EchoVerse collabs and merchandising.
