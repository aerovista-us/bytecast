# 🌾 Phase 2: Growth Protocol – The Art Localized

**Division:** Lumina Creative Media  
**Supporting:** EchoVerse Audio • SkyForge Creative Studios • Vespera Publishing  

## 🎯 Objective
Expand from narrative awareness to creator activation. Build a network of regional artists under AeroVista’s shared identity of “localized creativity.”

## Core Components
1. **Artist Onboarding** – Launch AeroVista Artist Network on `/the-art-localized/join`.
2. **Spotlight Series** – Weekly features on creators using AI in local art.
3. **Sound + Vision Sessions** – Live AI + Art showcases via EchoVerse.
4. **Submission Portal** – Accept artwork, remixes, and video collaborations.

## Metrics
- 100+ artist signups in 60 days  
- 10k+ weekly impressions  
- 5 regional showcases  
- 1 partner per region (gallery or maker space)

## Tagline
“From our neighborhoods to the network — creativity belongs to everyone.”
