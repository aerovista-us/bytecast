# 🌐 Ecosystem Integration Overview

## Goal
Unify AeroVista divisions under a self‑sustaining creative economy, where every division drives tangible outcomes.

| Division | Function | Conversion Mechanism |
|-----------|-----------|----------------------|
| Lumina Creative Media | Marketing & exposure | Branded collaborations, sponsored features |
| EchoVerse Audio | Music licensing | Sync deals, streaming royalties |
| Nexus TechWorks | Platform infrastructure | Creator dashboards, analytics, payments |
| Summit Learning | Education & training | Paid courses, workshops, certifications |
| Vespera Publishing | Media & literature | Print‑to‑earn books, creator anthologies |
| Horizon Aerial & Visual | Visual capture | Paid shoots, gallery partnerships |

## Integration Layer
NXCore acts as the foundation, hosting data, analytics, and commerce APIs linking all creative functions.
