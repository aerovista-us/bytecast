Every decision.

But eventually...

you hit a limit.

[pause]

Time.

Energy.

Attention.

Focus.

adding energy to the system.

[pause]

Different perspectives.

Different skills.

Different ideas.

Growth becomes exponential...

instead of linear.

Messaging.

Control reduces risk.

[CORE REALIZATION]

Designing environments...
