Is AeroVista a good fit for how you work?

AeroVista moves fast, but clean. If you like shipping small, documenting as you go, and self-unblocking, you’ll probably thrive here. If you need constant direction or enjoy surprise refactors… you’ll hate it.

🧩 Take the Crew Check (3 min) 📖 Read About AeroVista Pass = you’ve earned a follow-up request. Not automatic membership.

What the Crew Check proves

Not “IQ.” It’s signal on the stuff that matters in a real repo.

  • Competency: you can read and follow rules without needing handholding
  • Self-guidance: you know how to unblock yourself and ask clean questions
  • Commitment: you finish the flow and take the next step

How AeroVista works

These are the habits that keep shipping clean and safe.

Rule of thumb: one task → one PR

✅ You’ll like it here if…

  • You prefer small, reviewable changes over giant rewrites
  • You can explain: what changed, why, and how to test
  • You respect scope: in + out + acceptance criteria
  • You ask “where’s source of truth?” before building on vibes

🚫 You’ll hate it here if…

  • You like “while I’m here I refactored 12 things”
  • You prefer shipping without test notes or reproduction steps
  • You need live back-and-forth direction to make progress
  • You treat boundaries (secrets/infra) casually
Important: Passing the Crew Check doesn’t grant access control. It generates a clean “signal packet” you can post (score + lane + intro). If it matches current needs, founder reaches out.
Tip: If you just want to watch, you can skip everything and follow public drops.
