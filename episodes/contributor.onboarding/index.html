How we work

Simple rules that keep the system stable — and the crew sane.

Ship clean

Small is professional

One task, one PR, one deliverable. Keep changes reviewable and easy to validate.

  • No stealth refactors.
  • No “while I’m here…” overhauls.
  • Log extra ideas as new tasks.

Ask “WHERE” first

Before building: identify source-of-truth, verification steps, and safety boundary.

  • Source of truth: repo/folder/file
  • Verify: local + preview
  • Boundary: safe lane (A/B/C)

Honest helper energy

Say what you know, what you don’t, and what you need — early, not late.

  • Blocked on / tried / got / next guess.
  • No silent guessing in risky areas.
  • Document as you go — short notes.
