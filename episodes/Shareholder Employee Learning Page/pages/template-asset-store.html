5. Template + Asset Store

Every repeated AV process can become a downloadable product.

What this play is

A shop for templates, checklists, starter kits, campaign packs, HTML blocks, creative prompts, trackers, and printable assets.

Simple read: Turn repeated AV workflows into sellable templates: launch checklists, booth kits, campaign packs, music-drop assets, and trackers.

Why it matters

  • Low delivery cost after creation.
  • Makes internal workflows profitable.
  • Supports creators who are not ready for full service.
  • Creates a ladder from DIY product to paid AV implementation.

Build sequence

  1. Identify 10 repeated assets already being created.
  2. Package the first 3 as clean downloads.
  3. Create thumbnails, product copy, and usage instructions.
  4. Sell through AV store, Gumroad-style flow, or existing ecommerce path.
  5. Bundle templates into higher-value kits.

How to explain it

To a potential shareholder

Templates turn delivery knowledge into scalable inventory.

To a future employee

This is the “don’t waste good work” lane: if a team builds it twice, someone should consider productizing it.

How this feeds the AV ecosystem

This play should not live alone. It should create reusable assets, stronger demos, cleaner process notes, and a clearer sales path for the rest of AeroVista.
