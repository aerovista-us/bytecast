Standard Scoring Layer

Score Layer	What It Measures

Signal Count	Number of independent developments supporting the cluster.
Cross-Domain Alignment	Whether the signal appears across regulation, banking, markets, infrastructure, geopolitics, and technology.
Institutional Weight	Whether the actors are systemically important: Fed, OCC, SEC, Treasury, JPMorgan, BlackRock, major utilities, hyperscalers, sovereigns.
Velocity	Whether developments are accelerating across days/weeks.
Fragility / Risk	Whether the cluster creates systemic stress, liquidity mismatch, chokepoints, or control-surface risk.
Actionability	Whether the signal creates a watchable trigger, business opportunity, or strategic positioning move.


Phase Labels

Phase	Meaning

Emerging	Early signals, weak adoption, mostly conceptual.
Building	Multiple independent signals, early products, regulatory attention.
Active	Real adoption, institutional participation, repeated confirmations.
Breakout	System-level impact, market repricing, policy response, or infrastructure buildout.
Stress	Risk phase: liquidity shock, chokepoint disruption, regulatory crackdown, or forced deleveraging.
